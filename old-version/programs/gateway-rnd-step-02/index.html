Gateway to R&D (Advanced)



Hi,

The Gateway to R&D program's goal is to make students ready for product development roles in product companies. This program gives them lots of confidence and helps them realize their inherent potential to become top R&D engineers.

The second step (starting on Mar 10th, 2021) in this journey is for 3 months. The goal is to nurture you to Building a Modern Computer From First Principles. Students gain expertise in computer science internals and implement software used regularly.

We will look into how to build a computer from scratch. Understand the foundation of internet applications, by developing WebApps, involving the full stack. This is a hand-on practice based method for learning. Covering the core concepts, by implementing different applications. Weekly 3 days evening classes are conducted. To be part of this, you need to work 3 hours daily. You need to have a computer with a stable internet connection.

In short:
Gateway to R&D Online | advanced step - Mar 10th, 2020 | 3 months | Weekly 3 days evening classes | Practice like a professional to become top R&D engineer
