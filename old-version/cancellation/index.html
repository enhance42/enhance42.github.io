Cancellation Policy



Fee Refunds: Once the fee is paid, it is non-refundable. However, if you are unable to attend the scheduled training, you may have the option to join the next available batch or reschedule your training, subject to availability.

Cancellation Notice: If you wish to reschedule your training or join a different batch, you must provide a cancellation notice at least 3 days before the start of the original training.

Rescheduling Availability: Rescheduling is subject to availability and at the discretion of the Training Institute. We will make reasonable efforts to accommodate your request, but we cannot guarantee availability in specific batches.

No-Shows: If you fail to attend the scheduled training without prior notice or cancellation, you will not be eligible for a refund or the option to join a different batch.

Change of Training Dates: The Training Institute reserves the right to modify or change the scheduled training dates. In such cases, we will offer you the option to join the rescheduled training or provide a refund upon your request.

Please carefully review this cancellation policy before making any payments. By proceeding with payment, you acknowledge and accept the terms outlined above.
