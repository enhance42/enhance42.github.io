Our process



We believe that when anyone regularly puts effort into doing a specific craft, he eventually becomes a master in craftsmanship.

Our goal is to make our students work on problems alone late into the night and sleep peacefully once the problem at hand is solved.

We create an environment for our students to become immersed in problem-solving using coding. This is our core strength.

We nurture their curiosity and enable them to solve their own problems using coding. And work on their ideas to make them real.

In this journey, we make our students create their own process. Students design their own process and use it regularly to enhance their capabilities.
